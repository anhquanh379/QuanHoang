The Post

The Post is a 2017 American political thriller film about The Washington Post and the publication of the Pentagon Papers. Directed by Steven Spielberg, starring Meryl Streep and Tom Hanks. The film depicts the struggle of journalists to publish government documents that revealed the role of the US in the Vietnam War.
